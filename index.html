Our Story

Built new.
Rooted deep.

The Carolina Farm Post was built from the ground up on land that has fed families for generations. Surrounded by working cattle and some of the most beautiful open farmland in the Carolina Piedmont, it's the kind of place that feels special the moment you turn down the driveway.

What We Offer

Everything your
day deserves

Brand new construction on historic farmland. Here's what's included.

01

The Main Barn

3,500 sq ft of soaring timber and warm lighting with plenty of room for dinner, dancing, and up to 175 guests.

02

Ceremony Grounds

Exchange your vows surrounded by historic stone chimneys, timber frames, and lush Carolina farmland. A ceremony setting unlike anything else in the region.

03

Bridal Suite

A beautifully appointed suite inside the barn, designed for the bride and her party. Natural light, vanity space, and a calm retreat to start your morning right.

04

Grooms Suite

A dedicated space for the groom and his party, tucked inside the barn. Comfortable and private, ready for the moments before you walk out to meet her.

05

The Pavilion

The attached Pavilion features barrel cocktail tables and an outdoor bar, a natural flow from the ceremony into the evening. It also serves as a covered backup for outdoor ceremonies.

06

Catering Kitchen

A fully equipped prep kitchen accommodates any licensed catering team. The serving window bar keeps the flow going between the kitchen and your guests all night.
